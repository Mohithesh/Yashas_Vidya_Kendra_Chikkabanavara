ADMISSIONS

We thank you for your interest in joining your children in Yashas Vidya Kendra. We would like you to visit the school and talk to our Admissions Counselor and academic coordinators to understand our school and make an informed decision for your children.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS :

➀ Birth Certificate Copy
➁ Caste Certificate Copy
➂ Transfer certificate [TC] Original
➃ Student Photo [1]
➄ Parents Photo [Father , Mother / Guardian]
➅ Aadhar card copy [Student , Mother and Father]
➆ BPL Card Copy
➇ Bhagya Lakshmi Bond Copy
➈ Marks Card Copy

❖ I std to Xstd :- Time: 8:45am – 3:30pm
